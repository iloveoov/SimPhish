Detecting Phishing Emails

Verify Identities

False identities are used to make you believe emails are from a trusted source to encourage you to divulge sensitive information. Look at the email address and ensure the sender is valid.

Beware of Urgency

Bad actors try to add a sense of urgency to the email so you don't have time to think. Always verify if it is from who you think it is.

Look but Don't Click

Hover or mouse over parts of the email without clicking. The pop-up text may look strange or not match the description of the message.

Check for Errors

Emails may also have spelling or grammatical errors. If anything raises suspicious, report it.
